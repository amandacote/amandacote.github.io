Get to know me

Hello, my name is Amanda. I am a senior at UMass Dartmouth and I am majoring in Graphic Design with a minor in Business.

I am mainly interested in designing posters, logos, and helping with brand identity. I create illustrations and paintings on the side as well. I am familiar with CSS, HTML, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and XD. I am a very hardworking person and will complete the projects that I begin.
