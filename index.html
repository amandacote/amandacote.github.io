Get to know me

Hello, my name is Amanda. I am a Junior at UMass Dartmouth and I am majoring in Graphic Design with a minor in Business.

I am mainly interested in creating posters, booklets, and illustrations. I am familiar with CSS, HTML, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and XD. I am a very hardworking person and will complete the projects that I begin.
