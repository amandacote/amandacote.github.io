Merging of Minds Book

Book that compares and contrasts Mary Oliver and Corita Kent

Full Book

Project description

This was a project for my Advanced Typography class and I had to create a booklet that compared and contrasted the lives and work of Mary Oliver and Corita Kent. It includes a selection of my favorite poems by Mary Oliver and paintings by Corita Kent. Quotes by them are included so you can see how their thoughts compared as well.

Role

Design and creation of the booklet

Challenge:

Deciding what information was important for the booklet. Also, coming up with a layout that made sense for Oliver and Kent.
